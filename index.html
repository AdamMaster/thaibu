How Thaibu Protects You from Unreliable Partners

Build business relationships with confidence in Thaibu’s secure ecosystem

  • Manual Verification

    Every profile undergoes moderation. We remove users flagged for mass registrations or suspicious activity.

  • Participation Limits

    One individual can serve as a partner or director in up to three companies. Beyond that, their account is automatically restricted.

  • Transparency Monitoring

    We track all new partnerships and enforce compliance with our rules—this helps eliminate fake affiliations and gray-area schemes.
